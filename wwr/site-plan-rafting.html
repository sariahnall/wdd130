Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our website is to create a dynamic online hub that connects adventure seekers with outstanding water rafting experiences worldwide. We aim to provide comprehensive destination guides, while fostering a community through user reviews. Our goal is to simplify the planning process, helping visitors find tailored rafting trips that match their skills and interests, ultimately inspiring safe and memorable adventures.

Audience

Our target audience for the World Wide Water Rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts aged 25 to 45, typically active professionals or young families. They seek exhilarating rafting experiences suitable for various skill levels, along with reliable information on destinations. Currently, they lack a comprehensive platform that combines destination guides and community reviews tailored to their needs. Most access our site via mobile devices for on-the-go planning, while also using desktops for detailed trip coordination.
